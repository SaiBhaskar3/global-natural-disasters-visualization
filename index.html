Global Natural Disasters Visualization

This interactive dashboard presents a comprehensive view of global natural disasters from 1900 to 2021. The goal is to enable users to explore disaster impacts across countries, disaster types, and over time.

By analyzing key metrics such as total deaths, affected populations, and economic damages, users can uncover patterns, identify major risk areas, and better understand the historical footprint of natural disasters worldwide.

Explore the Dashboard

  • Map View: Discover total deaths by country with an interactive world map.
  • Sunburst View: Explore the hierarchy of disaster types and their impact.
  • Bubble Pack View: Visualize major disasters based on death tolls and affected areas.
  • Dynamic Filters: Filter visualizations by year and disaster type for targeted insights.
